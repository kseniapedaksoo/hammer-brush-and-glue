Our Classes

Don't be hesitant or assume that your child lacks artistic ability. It's all about developing skills! Consider enrolling them in one or multiple classes. They'll have the opportunity to meet new friends and explore their creativity. Plus, they'll have some gorgeous artwork to keep, gift, or even sell!

If you need more details, you can reach us by phone at 090 7333333 or by email at director@hammerbrushglue.com.

Drawing and Painting class

The course curriculum covers the fundamentals of fine art, including the history of painting and various drawing techniques that children will master. The course is designed for children who do not have initial training or with minimal training. The program consists of four thematic cycles, which are :
Cycle I. Introduction. Still-life.
Cycle II. Drawing plants. Landscape.
Cycle III. Fundamentals of perspective.
Cycle IV. Drawing animals and birds.

  • Course Supervisor – Debry Swanson
  • Course duration – 1 year – Monday to Friday - 2 hours per Day.*
  • All supplies included.
  • This class is limited to 10 students.
  • Suitable for children 7-9 years old.
  • Bring your biggest toothiest smile!

*The next class starts in September 2023. Please, find more information about Schedule & Fees.

Cutting and Pasting class

Working with paper has great educational value, particularly in terms of a child's mental and aesthetic development. Designing can expand their horizons and foster a creative attitude towards life. Taking a course on creating paper products can also enhance a child's artistic taste. It teaches them to observe and identify key characteristics, which can be valuable skills.

  • Course Supervisor – Elisabeth Bennet
  • Course duration – 1 year – Monday to Friday - 2 hours per Day.*
  • All supplies included.
  • This class is limited to 10 students.
  • Suitable for children 5-6 years old.
  • Bring your biggest toothiest smile!

Woodworking class

Welcome to this exciting class where your youth will get to create various fascinating products using hand carpentry tools. They will not only gain valuable experience in working with different types of wood, but also learn how to use, sharpen, and maintain these tools correctly. By the end of the course, they will have acquired new skills, knowledge, and a good set of tools, and best of all, they will have made beautiful wooden toys with their own hands. These toys are eco-friendly, safe, and perfect for children. Join us today and enjoy the satisfaction of creating something unique and special.

  • Course Supervisor – Ron Swanson
  • Course duration – 1 year – Monday to Friday - 2 hours per Day.*
  • All supplies included.**
  • This class is limited to 7 students.
  • Suitable for children 7-9 years old.
  • Bring your biggest toothiest smile!

Our Story

The Hammer, Brush & Glue Learning Centre is a family-owned business that offers childcare classes to children aged 5 to 9 years old. It was established in 2015 to serve the families of Sandyford and nearby communities. Our friendly tutors are committed to providing genuine care and warmth to the children. The safety and security of the children in our care are of utmost importance to us, and we strive to make sure they feel happy and secure while under our supervision.

Our Tutors

Our qualified tutors strive for the most important accomplishment: from playing with pleasure to learning.

Elisabeth Bennet
Cutting and pasting class

Elisabeth has been working with children's groups at our centre for 8 years. She utilises a unique game-based approach that sustains interest in learning and inspires creativity in children. Elisabeth is a graduate of the Faculty of Decorative and Applied Arts at the London Textile University.

Debry Swanson
Drawing and painting class

Meet Debry, our leader, and motivator. A highly experienced teacher with over 9 years of teaching young artists. With a degree from the Dublin Academy of Arts, Debry's lessons are carefully crafted to ensure maximum benefit and interest for her students. She's also a member of the Union of Artists of Ireland since 2013. Debry has participated in and won various world exhibitions and competitions in fine arts.

Ron Swanson
Woodworking class

Ron has over 9 years of experience teaching and working with children, making him a true master in his field. He earned a "Decorative and Applied Art" degree from the College of Arts in 1998 and later graduated from the University of Technology and Design in 2010.
